# Metal Archives Scraper

## Overview

The **Metal Archives Scraper** is a Python-based data collection tool designed to systematically gather band and album information from [Metal Archives](https://www.metal-archives.com/). This project was developed as part of a **Business Intelligence initiative for Enorion**, providing structured, actionable data to support strategic decision-making.

By converting raw web data into structured datasets, the scraper enables Enorion’s team to monitor market trends, analyze competitive patterns, and optimize release and marketing strategies.

---

## Key Features

- **Band Data Collection:** Retrieves essential band information, including name, country, genre, and status.  
- **Full-Length Album Details:** Captures album title, release year, and review counts.  
- **Data Quality Management:** Handles missing or incomplete entries and ensures consistent output.  
- **Scalable & Automated:** Designed to efficiently process large volumes of data across multiple letters and pages.  
- **BI-Ready Output:** Produces clean datasets suitable for dashboards, reports, and advanced analytics.

---

## Business Intelligence Applications

The datasets generated by this scraper empower Enorion to:

- Identify and analyze trends in the global metal music scene.  
- Benchmark against competitors and evaluate market positioning.  
- Make informed, data-driven decisions for releases, promotions, and audience engagement.  

These insights help the team transform raw information into actionable strategies, strengthening Enorion’s market intelligence capabilities.

---

## Output

The scraper generates two primary datasets:

- **Bands Dataset:** Core information for each band (name, country, genre, status).  
- **Albums Dataset:** Full-length album discographies with release year and review metrics.  

These datasets can be integrated into **business intelligence pipelines**, visual analytics dashboards, and strategic reports.
